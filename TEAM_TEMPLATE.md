## How to Submit

1. **Fill this template completely**
2. **Collect team photos** (individual preferred, group as fallback)
3. **Place photos in** `/team_photos/` folder with filenames like:
   - `member1_john_doe.jpg`
   - `member2_jane_smith.jpg`
   - `member3_alex_kumar.jpg`
   - `team_photo_group.jpg` (optional)

4. **Email to Flinders University** with subject:
   ```
   National AI Competition Showcase Submission - [Your Team Name]
   ```

5. **Include in email:**
   - This completed template as text or PDF
   - Filled SUBMISSION.md (with demo URLs working)
   - Team photos attached
   - Screenshot of the app
