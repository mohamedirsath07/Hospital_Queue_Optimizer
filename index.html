About AI Hospital Triage System

What is this?

An intelligent medical decision support tool that uses AI to help prioritize patient care and find nearby hospitals.

How does it work?

  1. Describe symptoms in detail
  2. AI analyzes and assigns urgency level (Critical, Urgent, Semi-Urgent, Non-Urgent)
  3. View hospital recommendations nearby
  4. Navigate or call hospitals directly

Technology

Backend: FastAPI • AI: Llama 3.3 70B • Maps: Google Places

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This is NOT a replacement for professional medical advice. This system is a decision support tool for educational purposes. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals. In emergencies, call emergency services immediately (108/112).
